Guest Speaker: ISE Labs Vice President

On Tuesday, October 29, 2024, the Vice President at ISE Labs came to Application Engineering Club to deliver an engaging presentation about computer hardware. During the interactive session, students explored key semiconductor components such as CPUs, GPUs, SSDs, and other essential technologies through hands-on demonstrations and real-world examples that illustrated how these components integrate within modern computing systems.

The presentation sparked lively discussion as students asked questions about current industry trends, career opportunities in hardware engineering, and cutting-edge innovations in semiconductor technology. This valuable event provided students with deeper insights into the hardware foundations of application engineering while effectively bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world industry applications.
